SHIELD

SHIELD is the parent group of the Avengers that oversees Earth's defences against various entities that threatens the world.

Panda

Pandas are large black-and-white bears native to China, known for their bamboo diet and distinctive markings.

Red Panda

Red pandas are small, tree-dwelling mammals with reddish-brown fur and a bushy tail, native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.
